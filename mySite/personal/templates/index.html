About Me

Though I had always been fascinated by coding since I was a young child, I quickly realized that my skills were more technical in nature after starting my career in 2009 as a graphic designer. After witnessing the creation of a very basic website, I fell in love. After that, I was like a dog without a bone, searching for my next move. I switched my attention from learning front-end to back-end development. With the introduction of new languages, I kept learning.

Due to my genuine enjoyment of web/app development, multimedia, and design, I possess a clear, logical mind, a practical approach to problem solving, and the drive to see things through to completion.

When I'm eager to overcome challenges and have a sharp eye for detail, it's easier to accept criticism and learn from my mistakes when I have an open mind. I've built databases and websites successfully in the past, and I can work both independently and cooperatively. In addition to having excellent technical and people skills, I am open to challenges that will help me grow and learn new things.

I have over 8 years of experience as a professional full-stack developer, and I have also built mobile apps and developed my Android engineering skills.
